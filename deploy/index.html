Philly Craft Beer Festival

Established in 2007

2020

The Philly Craft Beer Festival, the event USA Today named one of the 10 Best Beer Festivals in the U.S., Forbes Traveler called one of the "Top 10 Beer Festivals in America," and winner of Philly Beer Scene's coveted "Best Beer Fest" award for three years in a row, is back again for another great celebration of craft beer and brewing. The Festival will showcase more than 50 of the best local and national craft breweries, serving 150 different beers. The event will be held indoors (in raw warehouse space) and outdoors (under tents adjacent to the building).
